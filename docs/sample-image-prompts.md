# Sample Image Prompts

Use these prompts to generate optional demo memory images. Square PNGs work best for the app.

Common style direction to keep across all images:

```text
Soft watercolor and gouache illustration, warm blush and cream palette, deep muted burgundy accents, gentle paper texture, nostalgic handmade feeling, soft natural light, tender family-memory mood, subtle imperfections, no text, no watermark, no logos, no photorealistic faces, no harsh shadows, square 1:1 composition.
```

## Prompt 1: Photo Album

```text
Create a square 1:1 image in this style: soft watercolor and gouache illustration, warm blush and cream palette, deep muted burgundy accents, gentle paper texture, nostalgic handmade feeling, soft natural light.

Scene: a parent and adult child sitting side by side on a sofa, looking through an old photo album together. The faces should be softly suggested rather than detailed. Include a few loose printed photos on the table, a warm cup of tea, and a cozy home background.

Mood: quiet gratitude, memory, tenderness.

Avoid: text, watermark, logos, photorealistic faces, sharp digital rendering, harsh shadows.
```

## Prompt 2: Kitchen Memory

```text
Create a square 1:1 image in this style: soft watercolor and gouache illustration, warm blush and cream palette, deep muted burgundy accents, gentle paper texture, nostalgic handmade feeling, soft natural light.

Scene: a parent teaching an adult child a simple family recipe in a small kitchen. Show hands mixing dough or stirring a pot, with soft steam, a folded towel, and a few simple utensils. Keep the people universal and gently abstract, not identifiable.

Mood: home, care, ordinary love.

Avoid: text, watermark, logos, photorealistic faces, cluttered background, harsh shadows.
```

## Prompt 3: Train Station Hug

```text
Create a square 1:1 image in this style: soft watercolor and gouache illustration, warm blush and cream palette, deep muted burgundy accents, gentle paper texture, nostalgic handmade feeling, soft natural light.

Scene: a warm goodbye hug at a small train station platform. Show two figures embracing with a suitcase nearby, a soft train silhouette in the background, and a few floating heart-like petals in the air. Keep the scene intimate and calm.

Mood: distance, love, see-you-soon.

Avoid: text, watermark, logos, readable signs, photorealistic faces, dramatic lighting.
```

## Prompt 4: Video Call

```text
Create a square 1:1 image in this style: soft watercolor and gouache illustration, warm blush and cream palette, deep muted burgundy accents, gentle paper texture, nostalgic handmade feeling, soft natural light.

Scene: a phone on a bedside table showing a simple video call silhouette between a parent and adult child. Around the phone, include a small lamp, folded blanket, and a tiny flower vase. The screen should show only soft shapes, no readable interface or text.

Mood: long-distance connection, gentle surprise, comfort.

Avoid: text, watermark, logos, real app UI, photorealistic faces, harsh screen glow.
```

## Prompt 5: Childhood Bicycle

```text
Create a square 1:1 image in this style: soft watercolor and gouache illustration, warm blush and cream palette, deep muted burgundy accents, gentle paper texture, nostalgic handmade feeling, soft natural light.

Scene: a parent holding the back of a child's bicycle seat while the child learns to ride on a quiet neighborhood lane. Make it feel like a memory, with soft edges and a slightly faded-photo quality.

Mood: encouragement, childhood, trust.

Avoid: text, watermark, logos, detailed faces, modern street clutter, harsh shadows.
```

## Prompt 6: Celebration Table

```text
Create a square 1:1 image in this style: soft watercolor and gouache illustration, warm blush and cream palette, deep muted burgundy accents, gentle paper texture, nostalgic handmade feeling, soft natural light.

Scene: a small family celebration table with a simple cake, flowers, warm plates, and two hands reaching toward each other across the table. The people can be partially visible, cropped naturally, and softly rendered.

Mood: anniversary, birthday, gratitude, family warmth.

Avoid: text, watermark, logos, readable cake writing, photorealistic hands, harsh shadows.
```
